About Garsen North Ward.

Garsen north ward Under the leadership of Dumba Semi is an electoral ward in kenya.it is one of the ward in Garsen Constituency in TANA RIVER COUNTY It has a population approximately 25,716and ward area of about Sq.Km1585.50.

    Garsen north ward is comprise of three location :-

  • Salama location.
  • Mwina location.
  • Ndera location.

These locations lie on the delta of River tana and the villages align themselve along River Tana. So that they can access water for irrigation and improve there life standard. The expansive delta is characterized by wetlands making the region suitable for Agricultural activities.During dry season it provides grazing ground and tourism attraction. The delta can be used by the county to reform the economic status of the region. The region is capable of feeding the whole county and export surplus to the neighbouring counties.

Why Us

Why Garsen North is best Area for Investment

01

Agriculture

Due to the fertility of the land in garsen north. the land becames suitable for irrigation. Rice growing in garsen north ward was done in large scale by TARDA and also in small scale by the residents especially in salama location. Water for irrigation came from River Tana which for years now it became dry due to river divergency. The scheme was able to produce approximately 600 tonnes of rice annually. Hence Garsen north ward which lie on the Delta of River Tana can be used to provide food to the county and other regions surrounding it.

02

Tourism

During dry season Garsen north ward becames cite for torist attraction.The area is rich in wildlife. Monkey, Zebra Antelepo and many other animals are available in Garsen north. The presence of River Tana encourage the growth of the pokomo culture and other communities surrounding it. The community they have unique dance and other cultural festive which greatly attract tourism for example The The Kitoko dance which is widely known by the Pokomo community.

03

Community Support

Pokomo people are know by there kindness in every place they go. The community is welcoming humble, caring and more of that is supportive. Any organization thats wants to invest in the region the community support them. The community embrace theme of change in the region this makes them to grow very fast in terms of agriculture.

Activities

Economic Activity

  • Rice Irrigation
  • SugarCane Production
  • Bee Keeping
  • Livestock Rearing
  • Mango & Bananas Plantations

Tarda Scheme & Small scales

Rice growing in Garsen North ward is practised both large scale and small scale. Small scale rice growing was practised by mainly Wema village and Hewani village due to the construction of community schemes.

The presence of River Tana which is known for its water capacity. Makes the area to be suitable for the growth of rice in the region. this is due to presence of water from the river. Another factor making garsen north unique its because lies on the delta of river tana which is fertile and suitable for agriculture

Production of sugarcane

Sugarcane produced in large and small scale

Large scale production of sugarcane was maimly done by the Sailon community And Tarda Scheme. And other community such us Mikameni, Kitere, Mwina ,Wema, Hewani, Vumbwe and other villages sugarcane growing was done along river Tana due to the presence of the water.

Production of Honey

Bee keeping is done in small scales by community groups

Bee keeping in Garsen north ward is done mainly by Vumbwe community. The community use both traditional beehives and modern. Due to the presence of water from river Tana almost every community in the region is associated by Bee rearing. Bee keeping is one of the recently growing activity in garsen north.

Milk and Meat Production

Livestock rearing is done in small scales but in large heards

Livestock rearing in the region is mainly done by the orma ,wardei and pokomo community around the region. The activity is practised in large heards of cattles.Due to the presence of River tana water is available for livestock after grazing especially during dry season.

Production of Mango & Banana

Mango production in Garsen north is done in large scale

Mango and banana planting is done along river tana due to the presence of water. All community in the ward participate in mango growing and planting of bananas. Because Banana is one of the traditional food of the pokomo people. Example the Nkumbu & Matoli are made from bananas.

Cultural Festivals

Celebrations are done every year

The community have many celebrations each year to unite them together. Celebrations occurs during marriage, Naming of a child and other celebrations. The council of elders known as the Gasaa they lead the people in the celebrations to make sure are successfull.

Gassa

Every village they have a council of elders known as the Gassa.

Gassa is a community meeting that is lead by the elders. This meetings occurs during special occassions. Also they occur if a matter needs the elders to solve it.

Kupfanga

Is a type of women's dance.

This is a special and unique dance to the pokomo people .The dance is for the women they dance if they are celebrating. And also Mens are envolved if they are welcomed by the women.
